More about me

How I started ›
I grew up both surrounded and fascinated by technology, I started out making simple games on Scratch with the help of my older brothers, this grew into a yearning to learn more about programming, as life went on, I branched into many different aspects of coding. I learnt HTML(how this website is up and running!), Python and Javascript as well as many other languages. I often find myself venturing into projects after seeing similar ideas online.
My goal ›
To learn more about the different aspects and roles in the tech industry, to become an easily communicative person, who can share ideas and collaborate effectively, as well as to build impactful software that makes a difference.
My motivations ›
I've always been fortunate to have supportive parents who encouraged my curiosity, and always did their best to provide for me and I never had to worry about finances, instead what drives me is a desire to create and remain curious, like my dad always used to say, "Stay curious."

The idea of being a 'Jack of all trades' has always been appealing to me, but I never resonated with the second part of the quote 'Master of none'. Although being good at everything is nearly impossible to achieve, I would like to at least try to be the very best at the areas I'm passionate about.
Where I see myself in ~1,000 days ›
Contributing to impactful softwares, collaborating with diverse teams, and continuously learning.
